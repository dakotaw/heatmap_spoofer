## Dutch Island (Rhode Island)

Dutch Island is an island lying west of Conanicut Island at an entrance to Narragansett Bay in Rhode Island, United States. The island is a part of the town of Jamestown, Rhode Island, and has a land area of 0.4156 kmÂ² (102.7 acres). It was uninhabited as of the 2000 census.
