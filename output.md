## Minuscule 523

Minuscule 523 (in the Gregory-Aland numbering), Îµ 145 (in the Soden numbering), is a Greek minuscule manuscript of the New Testament, on a parchment. Palaeographically it has been assigned to the 14th century. Scrivener labelled it by number 489. It was adapted for liturgical use, with full marginalia.
